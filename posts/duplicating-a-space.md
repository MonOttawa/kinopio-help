You can duplicate a space by exporting it as a `JSON` file, and then reimporting that file

## Export

`Spaces → Export → JSON`

![export-space](/assets/posts/export-space.png)

## Then Import

`Spaces → Import`

![import-space](/assets/posts/import-space.png)
